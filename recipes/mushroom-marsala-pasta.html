Creamy Mushroom Marsala Pasta

Description

This creamy mushroom Marsala pasta is a delicious vegetarian dish that's luxurious and simple to make at the same time. A rich, flavorful marsala sauce makes this pasta the kind of gourmet meal that you'll want to make over and over again.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium shallot
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tsp fresh rosemary, minced
  • 1 Tbsp flour
  • ½ cup Marsala
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 cup vegetable stock
  • ¾ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Optional: Buttered and toasted Tuscan bread, steamed broccoli

Recipe

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water according to the instructions on the package and reserve some pasta water before draining.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil and fry the shallot for 1 minute over medium heat until it softens.
  3. Next, add the mushrooms and rosemary to the pan and fry them for 8 to 10 minutes until they reduce in size and are slightly golden. Add the flour and stir to cover the mushrooms.
  4. Pour the Marsala and continue to cook for one to two minutes until the alcohol evaporates and you're left with a creamy sauce.
  5. Add the garlic and wholegrain mustard to the pan and stir to incorporate. Cook for another minute, then add the stock. Allow it to bubble for a minute, then add the double cream and cook for another 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Stir in the fresh parsley, then transfer the pasta to the pan. Give it a good stir until it's well covered in sauce and season to taste.
